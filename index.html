We Connect You To Rewarding Opportunities

Netpace Health is a leading healthcare staffing company that specializes in allied healthcare and nursing jobs. Whether a hospital, nursing home, or private practice, Netpace Health has the resources and expertise to meet your staffing needs. Equipping highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals with rewarding opportunities and ensuring that we match the right person with the right position. We help healthcare organizations find and hire the qualified professionals they need to provide the best possible care to their patients.

Taking care of the people who devote their lives to taking care of others

We utilize our extensive knowledge and resources to provide the best opportunities for Allied Healthcare and Nursing professionals. We can connect you with the perfect opportunity to pursue your career and lifestyle goals. We offer a comprehensive suite of services for our candidates and our team of experts is always ready to offer advice and assistance to help land the job of your dreams. Netpace Health is more than a comprehensive platform – we take care of your needs and set you up for future successes.

Taking the stress out of connecting high-quality care to patients

Netpace Health offers the most innovative recruitment services for qualified healthcare professionals. As clinicians, Allied Health and Nursing Professionals play an important role in modern health and social care services. By delivering high-quality care to patients and clients in a variety of different settings, they improve their health and wellbeing. By combining our staffing solution background with our tech expertise, we are able to utilize our extensive knowledge and resources to provide the best healthcare recruitment marketplace for a tailored experience.

Building communities around healthcare work

Netpace Health works as a conduit between clinicians and opportunities to address the healthcare staffing shortages. With Allied Health and Nursing professionals delivering high-quality care to patients and clients in the form of diagnostic, technical, therapeutic and direct health services - there has to be someone in their corner making sure they take care of themselves too. This is why we are building communities around work - ensuring that those at the heart of it, the Allied Health and Nursing professionals, are taken care of while they take care of us.
The following are included in Allied Health:
Allied healthcare staffing: We place qualified allied healthcare professionals in a variety of settings, including hospitals, clinics, and rehabilitation centers. Our allied healthcare staffing services include:
  • Physical therapy staffing
  • Occupational therapy staffing
  • Speech therapy staffing
  • Respiratory therapy staffing
  • Radiology staffing
  • Laboratory staffing
Nursing staffing: We place qualified nurses in a variety of settings, including hospitals, clinics, and long-term care facilities. Our nursing staffing services include:
  • Registered nurse staffing
  • Licensed practical nurse staffing
  • Certified nursing assistant staffing
